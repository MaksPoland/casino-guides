Recognizing Signs of Problem Gambling

It’s essential for players to be aware of the signs of problem gambling. Recognizing these signs early can help you or someone you know take the necessary steps towards safer gambling. Some signs to look for include:

  • Feeling anxious or irritable when trying to cut back on gambling.
  • Borrowing money to gamble or escaping financial obligations.
  • Neglecting personal or professional responsibilities in favor of gambling.
  • Frequently chasing losses or experiencing a compulsion to gamble.

If you notice any of these signs in yourself or someone close to you, it’s important to seek help. Various organizations provide support and guidance for individuals struggling with gambling addiction.
